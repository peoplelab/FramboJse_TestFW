							   SNIPPET HANDLER WIKI 
							--------------------------

********************************************************************************************


									SYNTAX:
							--------------------------

<snp_NAME  class="myclass" id="myid" anyattr="anyvalue" data-pars=" <data-pars values> " />

"myclass" (without class)					=> pbAttr[0]	|	snippet_handler will render these values "directly" as Attributes in the "root" tag
id="myid" anyattr="anyvalue" ...			=> pbAttr[1]	|

<data-pars values>											|	snippet_handler will compute (and transform) these values in the rendering process.
<key_1> := '<value_1>'										|
||															|
<key_2> := '<value_2>'										|
||															|
...															|
||															|
<key_n> := '<value_n>'										|
															|
PS => <value_x> can be written as {{value_n}}, forcing a 	|
	new computation by the template_controller.				|


Example:   <snp_rtxt_tshType class="myclass" id="myid" anyattr="anyvalue" data-pars="mykey1:='myvalue1' || mykey2:='{{myvalue2}}'" />
			will be rendered as :
			<span class="myclass" id="myid" anyattr="anyvalue"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-time"></span>text</span>
			-> "text" is the computation result of "data-pars" elements
